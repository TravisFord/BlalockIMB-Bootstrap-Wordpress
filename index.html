Blalock's International Mixed Martial Arts & Boxing Academy

Instructor-Chet Blalock

Chet Blalock is the owner of Blalock's International Mixed Martial Arts and Boxing Academy. Chet has had the opportunity to train with some of the greats in martial arts, is a former world class champion kickboxer, and has trained many champions in all aspects of combat sports. He is a 3rd degree black belt in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu under MMA pioneer and legend Allan Goes. He is also a seventh degree black belt in Joe Lewis Fighting Systems and a Master Certified Instructor of Muay Thai, Boxing, Jun Fan Jeet Kune Do and Filipino Kali/Eskrima.

Martial Arts Classes

Under Joe Lewis' and Bruce Lee's teachings, We follow Bruce Lee’s rejection of following a rigid singular style in favor of the more fluid and flexible approach of training in multiple styles. Students can keep what works for them, and discard the rest. The classes offered at Blalock's IMB include:

- Muay Thai/Kickboxing
- Boxing
- MMA-Mixed Martial Arts
- Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu/ Wrestling
- Jeet Kune Do
- Kali/Eskrima
- Self Defense
- Fitness Kickboxing for men, women, and children

-Training for beginners, advanced, & MMA competitors

-Classes designed for pre-teen, teen, & adults

-MMA Fitness for self-defense & total training

single martial art style is applicable to each and every person."

About Blalock's IMB Academy

Blalock's IMB MMA is located in the Fort Oglethorpe area, in Georgia. Martial arts training include classes in MMA, Muay Thai, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Boxing, Jeet Kune Do, Eskrima, Self Defense, as well as fitness/weight loss programs. Classes are for men, women, and children, come out and try a class!
